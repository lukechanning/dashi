# Dashi — Store Listing Copy

## App Name

Dashi

## Tagline

A quiet place to keep your days in order.

## Short Description (< 80 chars)

A calm daily guide — write what matters, check it off, and move on.

## Long Description

Dashi is a guide to your day, not a system to manage. Open it, write down what you need to do, check things off, close it. That's a complete use. There's no right way to use it — and no way to use it wrong.

Each morning your daily page is waiting: what's due today, anything that carried over from before, and your habits running quietly in the background. If you want to drop into a distraction-free focus session with a Pomodoro timer, that's there too. At the end of the day, a gentle reflection check-in helps you close things out and let go.

If you want more structure, it's there when you need it. Todos can live under projects, projects under goals — so the small stuff can connect to the bigger picture if that's useful. Or skip all that and just write down what you need to do today. Both work fine. You can also define Chains — sequences of steps for routines or multi-part tasks — and work through them one at a time.

Dashi is invite-only and deliberately unhurried. It won't ask for your attention or push you toward a streak. It's meant to feel like a quiet companion, not a boss.

## Features

- Daily page that shows exactly what's on for today
- Carryover for past-due items — reschedule, dismiss, or just do them
- Focus sessions — pick up to 3 tasks and enter a distraction-free Pomodoro session
- Reflection — a quiet end-of-day check-in to close things out
- Habits that slot into your daily flow automatically
- Chains — define a sequence of steps and work through them one at a time
- Goals and projects if you want the bigger picture connected
- Activity heatmap to notice your own momentum
- Upcoming view for a look ahead
- Magic link sign-in via Resend — no password to forget
- Works well on phone or desktop
